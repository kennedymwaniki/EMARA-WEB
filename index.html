Welcome to Emara Hotel!

Nestled in the heart of Sagana, Kirinyaga County, Emara Hotel is a haven of tranquility and luxury, offering you an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Our hotel boasts several fully furnished rooms, each designed with your comfort in mind.
When it comes to pampering our guests, our room service is second to none. Our dedicated staff is always ready to assist you with any request, ensuring your stay is as comfortable as possible. Whether it's a late-night snack or a rejuvenating cup of coffee, we are here to cater to your needs.

Experience the best of Sagana at Emara Hotel – where luxury meets hospitality.
